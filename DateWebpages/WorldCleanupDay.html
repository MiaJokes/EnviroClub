Celebrate cleaning up our planet, one piece of trash at a time on September 21st!

How Can I Get Involved?

World Cleanup Day is an annual global event that brings together millions of people to clean up litter and waste from our environment. By participating, you can help reduce pollution, protect wildlife, and improve the overall health of our planet. Whether you join an organized cleanup, volunteer your time, or organize your own event, your contribution matters.

Every year, millions of tons of waste pollute our oceans, rivers, and forests. This pollution harms wildlife, damages ecosystems, and contributes to climate change. By taking part in World Cleanup Day, you can help address this issue and create a cleaner, healthier planet for future generations.
