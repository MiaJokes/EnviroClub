Common Questions

How do I upload an image to be analysed?

Click the 'Analyse Photo' button at the top of the webpage and upload your image by clicking on the 'Choose File' button. Wait a few seconds and your feedback will appear.

What types of objects can I analyse?

Any! Just make sure the image of the object is clear and with preferably no background.
